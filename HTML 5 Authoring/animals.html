Top Five Recommendations For Walking Your Dog

  1. Bring extra bags for when your dog has to go.
  2. Walk at a nice pace. Don't stress your dog out.
  3. Bring water just in case.
  4. Try going different routes each time.
  5. If you dog gets tired while walking, let them sit down for a couple of minutes.
